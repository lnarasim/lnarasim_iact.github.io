Programs

Post Graduate Program in Computational Science

Designed specifically to address a growing need in the industry, the Post Graduate Program in Science in Computing is 11 months course which aims to give the candidates an insight into the world of academic and industrial computing research.

Post Graduate Program in Data Engineering

11-month Program that helps participants develop skills in Computer Science & Data Engineering needed to develop Big Data Applications. Also, gain skills in technical problem solving.

Post Graduate Program in Decision Science

Make the best decisions possible given the constraints of a problem; help organizations identify efficiencies and operate more effectively. This program was designed to meet the growing demand for computationally proficient decision scientists and operations research professionals.

Post Graduate Program in Data Analytics

PG Program in Data analytics is structured to meet the demands with the right skill sets and to enable the trainees to understand the full science of Big Data Analytics - to retrieve data, store data, clean the data, interpret, analyze the data, and more importantly, predict results for organizations
